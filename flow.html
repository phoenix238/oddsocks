DATA LAYER
localStorage — primary store (clients, jobs, invoices, expenses, settings)
Google Sheets (GAS) — background sync, 3s debounce after any save()
Google Drive — POD photos & receipt images, URL stored on job/expense
Google Calendar — events auto-added on job create, removed on done
